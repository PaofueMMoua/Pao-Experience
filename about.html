Welcome To My Life

My life has been weird as throughout elementary school I grew up with my friends without always being the best student. Through middle school I began taking music and began coding as through this time I began by learning simple languages to understand like python while I also gained some attention within my musical career by placing within top 5 players in F.M.C.M.E.A.
