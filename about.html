Welcome To My Life

Welcome to My Life. I am a 16 year old Hmong American student studying at Clovis High and at C.A.R.T. (The Center for Advanced Research and Technology). I've grown up as the youngest male within my family. My life has been weird as throughout elementary school I grew up with my friends without always being the best student. Through middle school I began taking music and began coding as through this time I began by learning simple languages to understand like python while I also gained some attention within my musical career by placing within top 5 players in F.M.C.M.E.A. (Fresno Madera County Music Educators Association). Then Covid 19 hit like a semi-truck.

From there all work needed to be done at home. Through Sophomore and Junior year I became more social and more accepting of myself but I dropped out of my music courses and did pick up making music ironically. Then through that whole time I came to where I am now.
